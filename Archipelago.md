# What's in this apworld ?

## Achievements are locations
There's 🏆 **643 achievements** in Cookie Clicker. **Your goal** is to complete 200, 300, or even 600 of them, depending on your yaml !

Out of these 643, 🏝 **624 are archipelago locations** (checks)

## Upgrades and buildings are items
🛠 **All regular upgrades** are archipelago items. Cookies upgrades and Prestige upgrades can still be acquired like you would do in vanilla.

In addition, most 🏗 **buildings needs to be unlocked** by receiving an AP item !

Finally, **filler items** play a big role in this apworld: some filler items can give you **x9999999 cookies** !! But be careful, there are also traps out there, and you could lose it all in the blink of an eye... 🍪💸

## Progression
Achievements are grouped into **"tiers" of difficulty**, so early items should not be locked between "Bake 1 bajizillion cookies". There's about 6-7 tiers, plus a few special ones (endgame, grandmapocalypse...)

## Options
Cookie Clicker is designed to last for weeks and months, which is not ideal when you play with other people. They mostly aim to make the game faster. Unlocking buildings can be a major roadblock as well, so take a look at Progressive Buildings and Plentiful Buildings !
